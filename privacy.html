Privacy Policy

Effective: April 2026

Data We Collect

VentureStack stores the financial data you enter (ventures, transactions, time logs, tax profile) in a secure Supabase database. Your data is tied to your authenticated account and protected by row-level security — no other user can access it.

Authentication

We support Apple Sign In and email authentication. We do not store your Apple ID password. Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth and Apple's identity services.

Receipt Images

If you choose to photograph receipts, images are stored in a cloud storage bucket associated with your user account. You can delete receipt images at any time by deleting the associated transaction.

AI Scorecard

When you use the AI Kill/Scale Scorecard feature, aggregated (non-identifying) financial metrics from your ventures are sent to OpenAI's API to generate recommendations. No personal information, names, or account details are included in this request.

Analytics

We may collect anonymized usage analytics (screen views, feature usage) to improve the app. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Subscriptions

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store. Payment information is handled entirely by Apple — we never see your credit card details. Subscription management is handled via RevenueCat.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at support@venturestack.app. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Our database uses row-level security policies ensuring users can only access their own data.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@venturestack.app.
