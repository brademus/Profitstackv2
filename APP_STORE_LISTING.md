## Description

See which of your ventures actually makes money.

VentureStack is the P&L dashboard built for people juggling multiple income streams. Whether you're trading futures, flipping products, freelancing, running a SaaS, or doing wholesale deals — finally see everything in one place.

VENTURE-LEVEL P&L
Add each business as a separate venture. Track revenue, expenses, and net profit independently. See which ventures are carrying the team and which ones are dead weight.

TRUE $/HOUR
Log time per venture and see your real hourly rate. Your card flips might gross $2K/mo, but if you're spending 40 hours, that's $50/hr. Your SaaS might gross $500 but took 2 hours — that's $250/hr. This insight changes how you allocate your time.

QUARTERLY TAX ESTIMATES
Aggregates ALL income streams, applies self-employment tax + federal income tax brackets for all 50 states, and tells you exactly what to set aside each quarter. Never be surprised by a tax bill again.

AI KILL/SCALE SCORECARD
AI analyzes your ventures by profit margin, $/hour, and growth trend — then recommends which to double down on, which to maintain, and which to wind down.

RECEIPT SNAP
Photograph receipts, tag them to a venture and category. Export everything as CSV for your accountant at tax time.

Free: 2 ventures, full P&L dashboard, time tracking, receipt photos.
Pro: Unlimited ventures, tax center, AI scorecard, CSV export.

Built by entrepreneurs, for entrepreneurs. No bloat. No bank linking required. Just clarity on your money.
